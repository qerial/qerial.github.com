What is Monster Energy?

Monster Energy is a bold and powerful energy drink designed to give you long-lasting energy and intense focus. Known for its iconic green claw logo and aggressive flavors, Monster fuels athletes, gamers, and night owls alike.

With a blend of caffeine, taurine, B vitamins, and unique flavor combinations, Monster energizes your body and mind to tackle any challenge.

Popular Flavors

Original

The classic Monster taste with a bold, sweet citrus flavor and the signature green claw logo.

Pipeline Punch

A tropical mix of passion fruit, orange, and guava, offering a refreshing and fruity energy boost.

Ultra Sunrise

Light and crisp with citrus and orange flavors, perfect for a refreshing energy lift without sugar crash.

Mango Loco

Sweet mango flavor combined with a hint of tart citrus for a uniquely tropical experience.

Zero Sugar

All the energy and taste with zero sugar — ideal for those watching their sugar intake.

Ingredients & Info

Monster Energy drinks typically include caffeine (about 160 mg per 16 oz can), taurine, B vitamins, ginseng extract, and carbonated water. These ingredients work together to provide energy and mental alertness.

Consume responsibly. Not recommended for children, pregnant or breastfeeding women, or individuals sensitive to caffeine.
